6 DAYS NAIROBI - SAMBURU - MT. KENYA - MARA LUXURY AIR SAFARI

6 DAYS NAIROBI - SAMBURU - MT. KENYA - MARA LUXURY AIR SAFARI

Day 1: Depart Nairobi by flight to Samburu National Reserve. Arrive and enjoy an introductory game drive en route to your lodge. Afternoon game drive before dinner.

Day 2: Full-day exploration of Samburu, famous for its unique wildlife and dramatic landscape. Look out for leopards, oryx, and crocodiles along the Ewaso Nyiro River.

Day 3: Fly to the Mt. Kenya region and transfer to a forest lodge. Enjoy bird watching, nature walks, or relax with views of the mountain forest ecosystem.

Day 4: After breakfast, take a scenic flight to the Masai Mara. Afternoon game drive and overnight at a luxury tented lodge.

Day 5: Full-day game drives in the Masai Mara with picnic lunch. Optional balloon safari or visit to a Maasai village.

Day 6: Morning game drive and breakfast. Transfer to the airstrip for your flight back to Nairobi. End of safari.

What's Included:

  • Accommodation as per the itinerary
  • All meals (Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner)
  • Transportation in a safari vehicle with a pop-up roof for easy viewing and photography
  • English-speaking driver/guide with knowledge of flora, fauna, and Kenyan culture
  • Minivan with communication equipment, safari accessories, and bottled water
  • Park entrance fees
  • Government taxes and fees
  • Pick up and drop-off transfers
