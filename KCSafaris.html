Kenya Cultural Safaris

Kenya Cultural Safaris

Kenya’s villages provide an ideal opportunity to immerse yourself in the local culture and way of life. Explore untouched Kenyan villages, spend time with ethnic communities, and actively participate in their daily routines. The village experience offers an authentic glimpse into the traditions of Kenya’s 42 tribes. You can visit northern Kenya’s El Molo villages near Lake Turkana to learn about the unique lifestyles of the smallest tribes, or head to Western Kenya’s Alego Nyangoma Kogelo village, the ancestral home of U.S. President Barack Obama. Additionally, explore the historic Swahili ruins of Gede, located further south.
