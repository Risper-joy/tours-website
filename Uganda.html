Uganda

Ugandan Tours

Uganda, known as the "Pearl of Africa," boasts a diverse range of tourist attractions, from the breathtaking landscapes of Bwindi Impenetrable Forest, home to endangered mountain gorillas, to the stunning Murchison Falls, where the Nile River squeezes through a narrow gorge. Visitors can explore Queen Elizabeth National Park, famous for its tree-climbing lions and diverse wildlife, or take a boat cruise on the serene Lake Victoria. Adventure seekers can also trek the Rwenzori Mountains, while cultural enthusiasts can experience the rich heritage of the Baganda, Karamojong, and other indigenous communities.

Uganda Travel Information

Accommodation

  • Kampala offers diverse lodging, from luxury to budget.
  • Hotels and resorts are available nationwide, including in National Parks.
  • Campsites cater to budget travelers.

Transportation

  • Entebbe International Airport is the primary entry point (45-minute drive from Kampala).
  • Airport tax is included in tickets.
  • Major airlines: Uganda Airlines, Air Tanzania, British Airways, Egypt Air, Ethiopian Airlines, Kenya Airways, and more.
  • Daily Nairobi connections available.
  • Domestic charter companies provide regional flights.
  • Roads are generally good; **4x4 vehicles are recommended** for upcountry/parks during the rainy seasons.
  • Rental vehicles (self-drive/chauffeur) are available in major cities.

Banking & Currency

  • International and local banks/forex bureaus are widespread.
  • Banking hours: **8:30 AM - 3:00 PM, Monday-Friday**.
  • No foreign exchange restrictions.
  • Currency: **Uganda Shilling (UGX)** – use **post-1990 bills**.
  • Credit cards (**AMEX, Visa, MasterCard**) are accepted at limited locations.

Climate & Clothing

  • Warm lowlands, cool highlands (**21°C - 31°C year-round**).
  • Wet seasons: **March-May, October-November**.
  • Light summer clothing with a **sweater/jacket** is sufficient.
  • Comfortable shoes for safaris; informal dress is common.
  • Sun hat, swimsuit, and umbrellas/windbreakers for wet seasons.

Health

  • **Yellow fever vaccination certificate required**.
  • Check for **typhoid, cholera, and hepatitis** advisories.
  • Malaria risk: take **prophylactics before, during, and after the trip**.
  • Avoid tap water; use **boiled or mineral water**.

Language & Photography

  • English is the official language; **Kiswahili and Luganda** are widely spoken.
  • Bring extra **film/batteries** or purchase them in Kampala.
  • Binoculars are useful for game viewing.
  • No charge for cameras, but there may be a **fee for video cameras**.

Security

  • Take **standard city precautions**.
  • Use **hotel safety deposit boxes** or keep valuables secure.
  • Avoid walking **late at night** – use taxis instead.

Sports & Activities

  • Sports facilities in **Kampala and Entebbe** (**squash, golf, tennis**).
  • Fishing on **Lake Victoria** and the **Nile River**.
  • White water rafting at **Bujagali Falls**.

Visas

  • Visa **exemptions** for citizens of **PTA, EEC, Japan, and USA**.
  • Visas are available at **entry points, including Entebbe Airport**.
