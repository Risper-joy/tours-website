7 Days Best of Tanzania Luxury Lodge Tour

7 Days Best of Tanzania Luxury Lodge Tour

Day 1: Arrival – Arusha
Arrive at Kilimanjaro International Airport and transfer to your hotel in Arusha. Briefing and overnight stay at the lodge in preparation for your safari.

Day 2: Arusha – Tarangire National Park
After breakfast, drive to Tarangire National Park for a game drive. The park is known for large herds of elephants and towering baobab trees. Lunch and dinner at the lodge.

Day 3: Tarangire – Lake Manyara
After breakfast, drive to Lake Manyara National Park. Enjoy an afternoon game drive along the lake and forested escarpment. Overnight at a luxury lodge.

Day 4: Lake Manyara – Serengeti National Park
Drive to Serengeti National Park via Ngorongoro Highlands. Enjoy a scenic drive and arrive for lunch. Afternoon game drive and overnight at the lodge.

Day 5: Full Day in Serengeti
Explore the Serengeti with game drives in the central and northern regions. Spot lions, leopards, elephants, and cheetahs. Return to the lodge for dinner and rest.

Day 6: Serengeti – Ngorongoro Crater
Enjoy a morning game drive then head to Ngorongoro. Visit a Maasai village (optional) before arriving at your lodge. Dinner and overnight at the crater rim.

Day 7: Ngorongoro – Arusha – Departure
After breakfast, descend into the crater for a morning game drive. Later, return to Arusha for lunch and transfer to the airport for your onward flight.

What's Included:

  • 6 nights’ luxury accommodation
  • All meals (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner)
  • Private transport in 4x4 safari vehicle
  • Professional safari guide
  • Game drives in 4 national parks
  • Ngorongoro Crater tour
  • All park entrance fees
  • Drinking water during safari

What's Not Included:

  • Airfare and visa fees
  • Accommodation in Arusha before Day 1 (can be arranged)
  • Tips and optional tours
  • Personal expenses and drinks
