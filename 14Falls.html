14 FALLS

14 FALLS

Fourteen Falls Day Tour
Get lost in the beauty of Fourteen Falls, a scenic 27-meter high cascade located near Thika Town on the Athi River. Named after its 14 distinct waterfalls, this spot is perfect for photography, boat rides, fishing, and birdwatching. Nature lovers and adventure seekers alike will enjoy the fresh air, lush surroundings, and thrilling boat ride close to the crashing falls.

Before reaching the falls, you can enjoy a quick detour at Chania Falls or visit the nearby Ol Donyo Sabuk (Kilimambogo) for a short hike, camping, or a visit to the historic tomb of Lord Macmillan.

What’s Included:

  • Round-trip transport from Nairobi
  • Entrance fees to Fourteen Falls
  • Guided tour and boat ride near the waterfalls
  • Optional visit to Chania Falls or Ol Donyo Sabuk

What’s Not Included:

  • Meals and drinks
  • Tips for guides and boatmen
  • Personal expenses and additional activities
