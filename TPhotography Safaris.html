Tanzania Photography Safaris

Tanzania Photography Safaris

Day 1: Arusha – Tarangire National Park
Begin your photographic safari journey with a road trip to Tarangire National Park. Arrive at the lodge in time for lunch, then enjoy a game drive until 6:30 pm. Spot large herds of elephants, cheetahs, lions, and other rare species such as oryx and kudu. Dinner and overnight at a luxury lodge.
Meal Plan: Lunch & Dinner

Day 2: Tarangire – Ngorongoro Crater
Early breakfast followed by a 4-hour game drive in Tarangire. Continue to Ngorongoro via the Olduvai Gorge for a quick archaeological visit. Descend 2,000 ft into the crater for a magical game drive with picnic lunch among lions, rhinos, and flamingos. Dinner and overnight at the crater rim lodge.
Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner

Day 3: Ngorongoro – Serengeti National Park
After a brief crater floor drive in the morning, head toward Serengeti National Park. Arrive by early afternoon and enjoy an evening game drive through the plains filled with lions, elephants, giraffes, and more. Dinner and overnight at Seronera Lodge.
Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner

Day 4: Full Day in Serengeti
Spend a full day exploring Serengeti’s varied landscapes. Track wildlife including leopards, wildebeest, elephants, and birds of prey. Capture stunning moments in their natural habitat. Dinner and overnight at your lodge.
Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner

Day 5: Another Full Day in Serengeti
Enjoy another day of wildlife photography in different regions of Serengeti. Catch a glimpse of the Great Migration (seasonal), and enjoy the golden light perfect for safari shots. Dinner and overnight at the lodge.
Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner

Day 6: Serengeti – Arusha
After breakfast, enjoy a short game drive en route before driving back to Arusha to end your safari tour.
Meal Plan: Breakfast

Program 2
Day 1: Arusha – Tarangire National Park
Depart after breakfast for Tarangire National Park. Arrive for lunch and enjoy an afternoon game drive. Tarangire offers rich photo opportunities with elephants, big cats, and unique species like gerenuk and oryx. Dinner and overnight at the lodge.
Meal Plan: Lunch & Dinner

Day 2: Tarangire – Lake Manyara National Park
Drive to Lake Manyara National Park. This small yet scenic park features tree-climbing lions and vast flocks of flamingos. Enjoy game drives and stunning landscapes. Dinner and overnight at a lodge overlooking the lake.
Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner

Day 3: Full Day in Lake Manyara
Spend a full day in Lake Manyara with morning and afternoon game drives. Photograph giraffes, baboons, and countless bird species. Dinner and overnight at the lodge.
Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner

Day 4: Lake Manyara – Serengeti National Park
Depart Lake Manyara and drive to Serengeti. Enjoy an en-route game drive and arrive at Seronera Lodge by afternoon. Begin exploring the wildlife corridors famous for migration and predators. Dinner and overnight at the lodge.
Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner

Day 5: Full Day Serengeti Safari
Set out early with packed lunch for a full day of photography in the Serengeti. Capture the majestic cats, elephants, and seasonal migration scenes. Return to lodge for dinner and overnight.
Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner

Day 6: Sunrise Safari – Serengeti
Begin at sunrise with an extensive morning game drive. Experience Serengeti’s golden light as wildlife stirs. Picnic lunch in the bush, then relax at the lodge. Dinner and overnight stay.
Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner

Day 7: Serengeti – Ngorongoro Crater
Drive to Ngorongoro via Olduvai Gorge for a short visit. After lunch, descend into the crater for an afternoon safari. The caldera is home to lions, rhinos, and Maasai tribes. Overnight at a lodge on the rim.
Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner

Day 8: Ngorongoro – Arusha
After breakfast, return to Arusha to conclude your photographic safari.
Meal Plan: Breakfast
