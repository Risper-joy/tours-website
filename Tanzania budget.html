6 Days Tanzania Budget Camping Safari (Manyara – Serengeti – Ngorongoro)

6 Days Tanzania Budget Camping Safari (Manyara – Serengeti – Ngorongoro)

Day 1: Arusha – Lake Manyara
Depart Arusha and drive to Lake Manyara National Park. Game drive through forests, swamps, and lake shore. Overnight at a public campsite near the park. Day 2: Manyara – Serengeti National Park
After breakfast, travel to Serengeti through the Ngorongoro Highlands. Arrive in time for lunch and a sunset game drive. Dinner and overnight at Seronera campsite. Day 3: Full Day in Serengeti
Explore the Serengeti on morning and afternoon game drives. Spot lions, cheetahs, elephants, and possibly witness the Great Migration. Overnight at the same campsite.

Day 4: Serengeti – Ngorongoro Crater Rim
Early game drive, then depart Serengeti and drive to the Ngorongoro rim, stopping at Olduvai Gorge (optional). Overnight at Simba Campsite overlooking the crater.

Day 5: Ngorongoro Crater
Descend into the crater for a full-day game drive with picnic lunch. Explore the caldera filled with rich wildlife. Return to camp for overnight.

Day 6: Ngorongoro – Arusha
After breakfast, return to Arusha with optional souvenir shopping en route. Arrive by early afternoon.

What's Included:

  • 5 nights camping in national park campsites
  • All park and crater fees
  • Meals and bottled water
  • Transport in safari vehicle with pop-up roof
  • English-speaking guide and cook
  • Tents, mattresses, and cooking gear

What's Not Included:

  • Sleeping bags
  • Flights and visa fees
  • Optional visit to Olduvai Gorge
  • Tips, drinks, and souvenirs
