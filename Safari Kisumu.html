Safaris From Kisumu

Kisumu, Kenya's third-largest city after Nairobi and Mombasa, is located on the shores of Lake Victoria, the world's second-largest freshwater lake. While Western Kenya is not a major tourist hub and remains free from the usual safari crowds, Kisumu’s location makes it an attractive starting point for both budget and luxury travelers. With Kisumu International Airport now fully operational, many travelers in the region prefer to begin their journey here. The majority of Kisumu’s residents are from the Luo community, known for their fishing skills, making it synonymous with the region's fish industry, much like the Maasai are associated with cattle.
