Hell's Gate

Hell’s Gate National Park Day Tour
Embark on a thrilling day trip to Hell’s Gate National Park, where adventure meets nature in the heart of the Great Rift Valley. Choose between a guided game drive or a cycling safari through dramatic gorges, towering cliffs, and geothermal hot springs. This unique park allows visitors to walk or bike among zebras, giraffes, and antelopes, all set against a backdrop of extinct volcanoes and volcanic plugs.

Highlights include the famous Fischer’s Tower, Olkaria geothermal station, and Njorowa Gorge — all part of a raw, volcanic landscape that promises breathtaking views and adrenaline-filled exploration.

What’s Included:

  • Round-trip transport from Nairobi
  • Park entrance fees
  • Optional bike rental and guided walking safari
  • Visit to geothermal hot springs and volcanic formations
  • Photography stop at Great Rift Valley viewpoint

What’s Not Included:

  • Bike rental (if not included in package)
  • Meals and drinks
  • Tips and personal expenses
