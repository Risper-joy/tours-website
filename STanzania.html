SOUTHERN TANZANIA SAFARIS

SOUTHERN TANZANIA SAFARIS

Please note that Southern Tanzania Lodge safaris and tailor made tours and holidays can either depart from Nairobi or Arusha. Departure from Nairobi can either be in the morning with lunch in Arusha before commencement of safari or a night can be spent in Arusha before commencing the safari the next day in the morning. There is also an option for late arrivals in Nairobi to leave in the afternoon (1400 Hrs) and proceed to Arusha for dinner and overnight and commence the safari in the next day in the morning. Overnight accommodation and meals in Arusha is not included in our costs but we can suggest hotel accommodation and quote for the same.
