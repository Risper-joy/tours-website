BWINDI GORILLA TREKS

BWINDI GORILLA TREKS

Bwindi Impenetrable Forest Gorilla Trekking

Bwindi Impenetrable National Park, located in southwestern Uganda, is one of the best places in the world to experience the majestic mountain gorillas in their natural habitat. This UNESCO World Heritage Site is also home to over 120 other species of mammals and 350 species of birds, making it a prime destination for wildlife enthusiasts. We offer two exciting programs — a short and immersive 3-day trek, or an extended and more explorative 5-day journey.

3-Day Gorilla Trekking Program

  • Day 1: Depart Kampala or Entebbe for Bwindi, passing through scenic countryside and equator stopovers. Lunch en route. Arrive in the evening for dinner and overnight at the lodge.
  • Day 2: Early breakfast followed by a briefing at the park headquarters. Embark on an unforgettable gorilla tracking experience, which can take 2–6 hours depending on the location of the gorilla family. Return to lodge for rest, dinner, and overnight.
  • Day 3: Breakfast and return journey to Kampala or Entebbe with lunch stop en route. End of the tour.

5-Day Gorilla Trekking & Cultural Encounter Program

  • Day 1: Depart Kampala or Entebbe for Bwindi Impenetrable Forest, with scenic stops at the Equator and local craft shops. Overnight at lodge.
  • Day 2: Rest and acclimatization. Optional guided nature walk or visit to the nearby Batwa community. Overnight stay.
  • Day 3: Gorilla trekking day. After briefing, set off into the forest for an incredible encounter with a gorilla family. Spend up to one hour observing and photographing them. Return for dinner and overnight.
  • Day 4: Optional second gorilla trek or cultural village walk to experience Batwa traditions, dances, and storytelling. Return to the lodge for overnight.
  • Day 5: After breakfast, return to Kampala or Entebbe with a lunch stop. Drop-off at hotel or airport.

What's Included

  • Transport in 4x4 safari vehicle with English-speaking driver/guide
  • 2 or 4 nights’ accommodation in Bwindi (depending on program)
  • Meals as specified in the itinerary (Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner)
  • Gorilla trekking permit (one per person)
  • Park entrance fees and ranger guide fees
  • Drinking water during road transfers and treks
  • Community/cultural tour activities (in 5-day program)

What's Not Included

  • Visa and travel insurance
  • International and domestic flights
  • Additional gorilla permits (for second trek on Day 4 - optional)
  • Tips and gratuities
  • Alcoholic and soft drinks
  • Personal items (e.g., hiking boots, rain gear, camera gear)
