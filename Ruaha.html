3 Days Ruaha Luxury Lodge Adventures

3 Days Ruaha Luxury Lodge Adventures

Day 1: Dar es Salaam – Ruaha National Park
Fly or drive from Dar es Salaam to Ruaha. Arrive in time for lunch and enjoy an afternoon game drive in the baobab-filled wilderness. Dinner and overnight at the lodge.

Day 2: Full Day in Ruaha
Enjoy morning and afternoon game drives in search of elephants, lions, leopards, and large herds of antelope. Optional bush breakfast or sundowner. Dinner and overnight at the lodge.

Day 3: Ruaha – Dar es Salaam
After breakfast, enjoy a short game drive en route to the airstrip or road transfer. Return to Dar with lunch en route or at the lodge.

What's Included:

  • 2 nights in a luxury lodge
  • All meals and soft drinks
  • Daily game drives in Ruaha
  • Return flight or road transfer from Dar

What's Not Included:

  • Travel insurance and visa
  • Tips and alcoholic beverages
