UGANDA CULTURAL TOURS

Buganda Royal Sites

Buganda Royal Sites & Cultural Experience
Immerse yourself in the rich legacy of Uganda's Buganda Kingdom through a captivating tour of its most iconic royal sites. Begin at the Kasubi Tombs, a UNESCO World Heritage Site where Buganda kings are buried, and learn about sacred traditions passed through generations. Proceed to the Lubiri (Royal Palace) and Bulange Mengo, the kingdom's parliament, to explore Buganda's unique political structure and clan systems. Visit Nagalabi Buddo, the spiritual coronation site of Buganda kings, steeped in oral legends and ancestral rituals. To enhance your experience, enjoy interactive sessions with royal guards, traditional drumming and dance performances, and storytelling by local elders who share Buganda's rich oral history.
