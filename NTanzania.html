NOTHERN TANZANIA SAFARIS

NOTHERN TANZANIA SAFARIS

Kindly note that Tanzania lodge safaris and holiday tours may begin from either Nairobi or Arusha. If starting from Nairobi, guests can choose to travel in the morning and stop for lunch in Arusha before starting the safari, or opt to spend a night in Arusha and begin the northern circuit safari the following morning. For travelers arriving in Nairobi later in the day, there is also an option to depart at 2:00 PM, head to Arusha for dinner and an overnight stay, and start the safari the next morning. Please be aware that accommodation and meals in Arusha are not part of our tour package, but we are happy to recommend hotels and provide quotes upon request.
