5 Days – Manyara, Ngorongoro, Serengeti Luxury Lodge Safari

5 Days – Manyara, Ngorongoro, Serengeti Luxury Lodge Safari

Day 1: Arusha – Lake Manyara
Depart from Arusha after breakfast and drive to Lake Manyara National Park. Arrive in time for lunch at the lodge. In the afternoon, enjoy a game drive in the park known for its flamingos, tree-climbing lions, and rich birdlife. Dinner and overnight at the lodge.

Day 2: Lake Manyara – Serengeti National Park
After breakfast, depart for Serengeti through the scenic highlands of Ngorongoro. Arrive for lunch at a luxury lodge in central Serengeti. In the afternoon, take part in a game drive across the endless plains. Dinner and overnight at the lodge.

Day 3: Full Day in Serengeti
Spend a full day in the Serengeti with morning and afternoon game drives. Witness the Big Five and, depending on the season, the Great Migration. Enjoy meals and luxury accommodation at the lodge.

Day 4: Serengeti – Ngorongoro Crater
After breakfast, enjoy a final game drive en route to Ngorongoro. Descend into the crater for a short game viewing experience. Later, proceed to your lodge on the crater rim for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 5: Ngorongoro – Arusha
After breakfast, begin your journey back to Arusha. Arrive in time for lunch and transfer to your preferred location or onward departure.

What's Included:

  • 4 nights’ luxury lodge accommodation
  • All meals (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner)
  • Transport in a 4x4 safari vehicle with pop-up roof
  • Professional English-speaking safari guide
  • Park entry and crater service fees
  • Game drives in Lake Manyara, Serengeti, and Ngorongoro
  • Bottled drinking water during safari
  • Transfers from Arusha/Nairobi

What's Not Included:

  • International/domestic flights
  • Visa and travel insurance
  • Tips and gratuities
  • Alcoholic beverages and personal expenses
  • Optional excursions not mentioned in itinerary
