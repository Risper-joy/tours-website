WHITE WATER RAFTING IN JINJA

WHITE WATER RAFTING IN JINJA

Begin your thrilling adventure with a pickup from your hotel in Kampala, heading out for an unforgettable full-day white water rafting experience in Jinja, along the iconic River Nile. Your day includes a light morning snack, hearty lunch, and refreshing drinks at the end of the adventure. Whether you're a first-time rafter or a seasoned thrill-seeker, the Nile offers options for everyone — from adrenaline-pumping paddling to a more relaxed ride where experienced guides take the lead.

Equipped with top-notch safety gear and led by expert rafting professionals, you’ll conquer some of the Nile’s most powerful rapids over a 25 km stretch. These world-class rapids, ranging from grade 4+ to 5, promise an exhilarating and safe experience — and you're welcome to skip the extreme side runs if you prefer a milder ride.

Remember to pack a swimsuit, shorts, and secure sandals or water shoes — you’re going to get wet! A change of clothes is a must, and you’ll find facilities available for keeping valuables like cameras safe and dry. Don't forget your sunscreen, sunglasses, and an adventurous spirit.

For those seeking a more extended challenge, white water rafting can also be arranged in Murchison Falls National Park, with a multi-day expedition from Karuma Falls to the base of Murchison Falls. Contact us for customized details on this epic journey.

What’s Included:

  • Round-trip transportation from Kampala or Entebbe
  • Park entrance fees
  • Guided game drive in safari vehicle
  • Kazinga Channel boat cruise
  • Services of a professional English-speaking guide
  • Lunch at a scenic lodge or picnic spot

What’s Not Included:

  • Optional activities like chimp tracking or crater lake visits
  • Tips and personal expenses
  • Drinks and snacks
  • Accommodation (for multi-day trips)
