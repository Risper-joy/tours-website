5 Days Serengeti & Ngorongoro Air Holiday

5 Days Serengeti & Ngorongoro Air Holiday

Day 1: Arusha – Serengeti (by air)
Take a scenic flight from Arusha to Serengeti. Enjoy a game drive en route to your camp. Settle in, have lunch, and head out for an evening game drive. Dinner and overnight in a luxury tented lodge.

Day 2: Full Day in Serengeti
Explore the central or northern Serengeti (depending on the season). Witness the Great Migration, big cats, or herds of elephants. Enjoy a bush lunch or picnic. Return to camp for dinner and storytelling by the fire.

Day 3: Serengeti – Ngorongoro Crater (by road)
Enjoy a morning game drive and breakfast, then drive to Ngorongoro with scenic stops along the way. Arrive at your crater rim lodge for sunset views and dinner.

Day 4: Game Drive in Ngorongoro Crater
After breakfast, descend into the crater for a full day of game viewing. Visit the Ngoitoktok picnic site and return to the lodge in the evening.

Day 5: Ngorongoro – Arusha (by air)
Transfer to the airstrip for your flight back to Arusha. Optional lunch or connection to onward travel.

What's Included:

  • Scheduled flights (Arusha to Serengeti & Ngorongoro to Arusha)
  • 4 nights luxury accommodation
  • All meals and park fees
  • Ground transfers between lodges and airstrips
  • Professional safari guide and vehicle

What's Not Included:

  • International airfare and visas
  • Optional Maasai village visits
  • Alcoholic drinks and gratuities
  • Personal travel insurance
