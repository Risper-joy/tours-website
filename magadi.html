Lake Magadi

Lake Magadi

Lake Magadi Day Tour
Escape to the heart of Kenya’s Rift Valley with a day trip to Lake Magadi — a surreal landscape of shimmering pink waters, vast salt flats, and steamy hot springs. Located about 120 km southwest of Nairobi, this soda lake is one of the most mineral-rich in the world and a haven for flamingos and other birdlife. Surrounded by dramatic escarpments and scattered acacia trees, Lake Magadi offers a unique blend of raw natural beauty and geothermal wonders.

Visitors can relax in the natural hot springs, explore the salt formations, enjoy scenic walks, and witness traditional Maasai culture in the surrounding villages. Ideal for photographers, nature enthusiasts, and adventure seekers, Lake Magadi promises an unforgettable off-the-beaten-path experience just a few hours from the city.

What’s Included:

  • Round-trip transport from Nairobi
  • Guided tour of Lake Magadi and hot springs
  • Park entrance fees
  • Opportunities for photography and bird watching
  • Visit to nearby Maasai community (optional)

What’s Not Included:

  • Meals and drinks not mentioned
  • Tips and personal expenses
  • Swimming attire for hot springs (bring your own)
