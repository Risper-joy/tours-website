Day Tours From Nairobi

Day Tours From Nairobi

Explore a variety of popular sightseeing tours and excursions in and around Nairobi. Whether you're looking for day trips within the city or tours starting from Nairobi, there’s plenty to see and do. Enjoy guaranteed daily departures from the airport, Nairobi hotels, guest houses, or apartments—with free drop-off at the airport, train station, or shuttle service point. Discover Nairobi’s top attractions through exciting day excursions and sightseeing adventures.

Popular Picks

NGONG HILLS

Embark on a thrilling ridge-top trek along the scenic Ngong Hills! Climb steep trails past wind farms and radio towers with breathtaking views of Nairobi and the Great Rift Valley. Spot buffalo near lush peaks and soak in sweeping panoramas of Maasai villages and wildlife. End your hike with a cool breeze at “Kona Baridi” before heading back to the city recharged!

View Safari Details

LAKE MAGADI

Unwind with a scenic day trip to Lake Magadi—Kenya’s hidden gem! Enjoy panoramic views at Kona Baridi, explore ancient history at Olorgesailie, and soak in healing hot springs. Spot flamingos, stroll the soda lake shores, and meet the Maasai for a rich cultural experience. A perfect escape blending nature, history, and rejuvenation—just a few hours from Nairobi!

View Safari Details

LAKE NAKURU

Experience the beauty of Lake Nakuru National Park in a one-day tour, a haven for flamingos, rhinos, and rare Rothschild giraffes. Spot leopards, zebras, gazelles, and baboons while exploring the Rift Valley Lake. Enjoy lunch at Lake Nakuru Lodge or Sarova Lion Hill, followed by a scenic drive back to Nairobi. Customizable options available for extended excursions.

View Safari Details

HELLS GATE

Explore Hell's Gate National Park on a day tour from Nairobi, with a scenic drive through the Great Rift Valley. Enjoy a thrilling 7-kilometer bike ride, followed by a 3-hour walking safari through stunning landscapes and hot springs. View unique landmarks like Fischer's Tower, extinct volcanoes, and abundant wildlife such as giraffes, buffaloes, and leopards. The park is also a birdwatching haven with 103 species recorded.

View Safari Details

14 FALLS TOUR

Discover the stunning Fourteen Falls near Thika, a 27-meter deep waterfall with 14 successive falls along the Athi River. Enjoy activities like boating, fishing, photography, plant identification, and birdwatching. Before reaching the falls, stop at Chania Falls for more scenic views. Nearby, explore Oldonyo Sakuk National Park for hiking, camping, and the tomb of Lord Macmillan.

View Safari Details

AMBOSELI NATIONAL PARK

Embark on an early morning drive from Nairobi to Amboseli National Park, located 260km from Nairobi, at the foot of Mount Kilimanjaro. Known for its breathtaking views and abundant wildlife, it's a prime spot to see large herds of elephants and explore diverse habitats.

View Safari Details

MT LONGONOT

Escape the city with a thrilling day hike up Mount Longonot. Trek through volcanic trails and soak in breathtaking crater views. Enjoy scenic drives across the Great Rift Valley. Perfect for nature lovers and adventure seekers!
