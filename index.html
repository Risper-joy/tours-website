Maasai Mara Game Reserve

Maasai Mara National Reserve is an area of preserved savannah wilderness in southwestern Kenya, along the Tanzanian border. Its animals include lions, cheetahs, elephants, zebras and hippos. Wildebeest traverse its plains during their annual migration.

Lake Elementaita

The Great Rift Valley Elementaita is located in Kenya at approximately 120 kms from Nairobi City. Elementaita boasts of Lake Elementaita with shores which are beautifully decorated by Pelicans and Flamingos and is revered as one of the unsurpassed world’s largest ornithological spectacles. If you want to catch a glimpse of this beauty you can visit the few campsites and lodges that sit on vast acres of beautifully manicured gardens on the shores of this great Lake. The lush breathtaking well manicured gardens with expanse scenery is an ideal spot for excursions for family trips, individual travellers, couples getaways, weddings, group events and team buildings
