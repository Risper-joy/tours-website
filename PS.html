Photographic Safaris

Photographic Safaris

Program 1: 10 Days / 09 Nights Maasai Mara / Lake Naivasha / Lake Nakuru and Samburu

This safari combines the beauty of Kenya's best wildlife parks with stunning landscapes. Explore Maasai Mara's famous Big Five, enjoy breathtaking views at Lake Naivasha, and discover the unique species of Samburu. A dream itinerary for any nature lover and photographer.
The safari actively monitors weather conditions to ensure the best opportunities for photographing the wildlife and natural beauty.

Program (10 Days):

  • Day 1: Nairobi – Maasai Mara Game Reserve. Drive through the Great Rift Valley with a stop at the viewpoint, continue to Maasai Mara for an evening game drive. Overnight: Lodge.
  • Day 2: Full day in Maasai Mara. Morning and afternoon game drives in search of predators and wildlife. Overnight: Lodge.
  • Day 3: Full day in Maasai Mara. Optional Maasai village visit or early morning hot air balloon ride. Overnight: Lodge.
  • Day 4: Maasai Mara – Lake Naivasha. Evening game drive at Hell’s Gate National Park. Overnight: Lodge.
  • Day 5: Lake Naivasha – Lake Nakuru. Enjoy a morning boat ride at Crescent Island. Evening game drive at Lake Nakuru. Overnight: Lodge.
  • Day 6: Lake Nakuru. Pre-breakfast game drive for leopards, followed by breakfast and lunch. Late afternoon game drive. Overnight: Lodge.
  • Day 7: Lake Nakuru – Samburu. Travel to the northern frontier, home to unique wildlife. Evening game drive in Samburu. Overnight: Lodge.
  • Day 8: Full day in Samburu with morning and evening game drives, including possible sightings of rare species. Overnight: Lodge.
  • Day 9: Morning and evening game drives in Samburu. Optional visit to Samburu village. Overnight: Lodge.
  • Day 10: Samburu – Nairobi. Morning game drive, then return to Nairobi with lunch on the way.

Program 2: 10 Days / 09 Nights Amboseli / Lake Naivasha / Lake Nakuru and Maasai Mara

Experience the vast savannahs of Amboseli, with views of Mount Kilimanjaro, followed by the serenity of Lake Naivasha and Lake Nakuru. The safari also takes you to the Maasai Mara, famous for its annual wildebeest migration and large predator populations.
This program includes flexible plans that monitor weather conditions for the best possible photographic opportunities.

Program (10 Days):

  • Day 1: Nairobi to Amboseli. Drive through the Athi Plains and into Amboseli National Park. Evening game drive with views of Mount Kilimanjaro. Overnight: Lodge.
  • Day 2: Full day in Amboseli with morning and evening game drives. Visit a Maasai village for close-up shots of the tribe. Overnight: Lodge.
  • Day 3: Full day exploring Amboseli’s wildlife, including elephants, zebras, and giraffes. Overnight: Lodge.
  • Day 4: Amboseli – Lake Naivasha. After lunch, proceed to Lake Naivasha and enjoy an evening game drive at Hell’s Gate National Park. Overnight: Lodge.
  • Day 5: Lake Naivasha – Lake Nakuru. Early morning boat ride and nature walk at Crescent Island, then game drive at Lake Nakuru. Overnight: Lodge.
  • Day 6: Lake Nakuru. Pre-breakfast game drive to search for leopards, followed by a second game drive in the afternoon. Overnight: Lodge.
  • Day 7: Lake Nakuru – Maasai Mara. Drive across the Great Rift Valley to Maasai Mara. Evening game drive. Overnight: Lodge.
  • Day 8: Full day in Maasai Mara with morning and afternoon game drives in search of predators and wildlife. Overnight: Lodge.
  • Day 9: Full day in Maasai Mara with optional Maasai village visit or hot air balloon ride. Overnight: Lodge.
  • Day 10: Maasai Mara – Nairobi. Early morning game drive, then return to Nairobi for lunch.
